About

Strategic vision, paired with execution discipline.

Michael H. Loechel is a Strategy, Innovation & Transformation Leader who helps global biopharmaceutical organizations define where to compete, how to win, and how to make large-scale change stick.

Credibility

With over 25 years of experience, Michael brings a rare combination of strategic vision and execution discipline — from shaping enterprise innovation agendas to leading complex, multi-workstream transformations end-to-end.

Track Record

$450MM+ in contract opportunities uncovered; a $200MM+ Mexican EHR market entry defined; a €35MM projected revenue increase — roughly 6–7x ROI — from a commercial capabilities advisory engagement; and 50+ transformational initiatives led across Commercial and R&D functions for organizations including Pfizer, Amgen, Johnson & Johnson, AstraZeneca, Novo Nordisk, Boehringer Ingelheim, and Merck KGaA / EMD Serono. Earlier in his career, Michael also drove $8MM in annual revenue as the number-two performer on a national sales team, and helped structure an international commercial partnership valued at over €100MM annually — direct commercial experience that grounds his strategic advice.
